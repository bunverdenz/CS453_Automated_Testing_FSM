Welcome to Melodize

Listening to your favourite tunes has become easier than ever thanks to the variety of streaming platforms available today. However, it is not so easy for the public to contribute to the music composition process due to the public’s lack of expertise in the field and easily available tools; as a result, music composition remains to be a task exclusive to music artists. We want to change that dynamic. We want to make music composition a task available to anyone.
